Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Low Quality Image Generator collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Data Collected

We do not require registration. We only receive the files that users upload to process images. We may also automatically collect analytics data (e.g., visits, pages, anonymized IP) to improve the service.

2. Use of Uploaded Files

Uploaded images are temporarily processed on the server and automatically deleted after processing (configured for deletion within 24 hours). We do not use your images for other purposes and do not sell files to third parties.

3. Cookies and Third Parties

We use cookies and third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics and Google AdSense). These providers have their own privacy policies.

4. Security

We apply technical measures to protect temporary files and connections (HTTPS). However, no system is 100% secure.

5. User Responsibility

Avoid uploading images containing sensitive or private data. Users are responsible for the content they upload.

6. User Rights

Since we do not store identifiable personal data beyond temporary images (which are automatically deleted), there is no permanent data to request deletion.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the date of the last revision will be shown at the top of the page.
