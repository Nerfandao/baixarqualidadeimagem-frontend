How Pixelation Works

Pixelation divides your image into small square regions and replaces every pixel in each region with the average color of that region. At 2x, blocks are 2√ó2 pixels ‚Äî barely visible. At 20x, each colored tile covers 20√ó20 pixels, creating large visible blocks. This server-side implementation produces consistent results regardless of browser or device.

Pixelation vs Other Effects

Pixelation is a spatial effect ‚Äî it doesn't change colors or add noise by itself. Combine it with JPEG compression for the classic meme look, or keep it clean for pixel art and censoring. When NOT to use pixelation
